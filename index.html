About Me

I’m a Postdoctoral Researcher Fellow at McGill University’s CFD Lab working with Prof. Wagdi Habashi,. My research focuses on multi-phase flow modeling with a special interest in droplet dynamics. I have a strong background in developing numerical methods and numerical codes within a parallel environment. I also have prior experience in HVAC system design and in the field of renewable energy.
